ABOUT MIKE

During my years of Public Service in Mountain View, I have been a vocal advocate of affordable housing and policies that will help ensure that Mountain View is a place where average people can afford to live. I have worked to balance growth so that we do not have an ever increasing number of people working here without the housing so necessary to help keep our roads from becoming gridlocked and our atmosphere full of carbon. I pursued a review of the city's transportation policies and worked to find "win-win" solutions to the issues that face our city.
