Running a campaign takes a lot of time, work and effort by a team of people.  There are lots of ways to get involved: hosting a small coffee or gathering of friends at your home to introduce Mike; placing a lawn sign in your front yard or window; precinct walking, and; helping with our email campaign by forwarding campaign emails to your friends and neighbors.  If you would like to join our team as a volunteer only, simply click on the "Volunteer Now" button.

It will come as no surprise that even in Mountain View, campaigns can be expensive.  If you would like to help support our campaign, please click on the "Contribute Now" button.  You can also volunteer on the "Contribute" page if you want to do both.
